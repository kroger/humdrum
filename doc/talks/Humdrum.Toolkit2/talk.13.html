SIGIR'99: Music Information Retrieval


What Future Should We Aim For?


  1. Large, accessible, high quality, well-documented, cheap, multi-use, downloadable musical databases.

  2. Powerful, open sources, easy-to-use software that raises few bumps in the learning curve.

  3. A community of scholars who can revise, discuss, and criticize evolving database materials.
